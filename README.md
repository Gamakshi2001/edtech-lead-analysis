# edtech-lead-analysis
Analysis of 9000 leads identifying phone number validation issues. 

## What did I find?
- 83 leads (~1%) had invalid phone numbers
- These leads showed **HIGH ENGAGEMENT**(upto 2000 minutes)
- **Common profile**: Unemployed professionals in Maharashtra region
- **Primary sources of their contact**: **Organic search** and **Direct Traffic**
- All 83 leads never converted

## What can be their impacts on business?
- These are highly interested but uncontactable due to issues with their phone numbers

## What would I recommend? 
- Real time phone number validation on all signup forms especially for
- Users from Maharashtra
- Organic search traffic
- Unemployed professionals seeking career support

## Tools used 
- Excel (filtering, sorting, analysis)

## What I learned?
- Identifying patterns in data
- Connecting data points for storytelling
- Make data driven recommendations
